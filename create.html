Making a Green Litter Box

DIY cat litter box designed to use with wood burning pellets.

Save on money, and time by switching to a green littler box alternative. Using wood pellets to create a green (compostable) litter. Using wood pellets as a cat litter by absorbing the liquid – along with the smell – and then crumble into sawdust. The sawdust ends up at the bottom of the litter box, while the fresh pellets remain on top. Sprinkle additional pellets on the surface to maintain the litter box. Feces are scooped away and removed daily.
